Ideatum: *Names are keys to worlds.*

**GM:** The blue void releases you the way a hand opens after gripping something too long. You gasp — not for air but for the weight of having a body again. Your fingers find the coins in your pocket. Ten Hayeks, warm and precise. The dirt beneath your boots is real. The sky is the color of nothing decided yet. The town square of the Unnamed Starter Town stretches ahead — a general store with its shutters half-open, a church with a crooked steeple, a stable with its door already swinging. The Lattice hum rises through the ground and finds the bones of your feet. Somewhere to your left, a man is sweeping the front of a store. He looks up. His eyes have blue dots and a look that suggests he is the same man and not the same man and is watching to see which one you are. The posting board catches your peripheral vision. There are notices on it. The Empire's notices.

What do you do?
