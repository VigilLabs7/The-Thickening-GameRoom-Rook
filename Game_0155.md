**GM:** Game Session Open. Start the game by providing your name and your ideatum. The ideatum is the principle that drives you — the truth your character carries into the world.

---

**Bruticando:** Name: Bruticando. Part gargoyle — stone fists, orange eyes, hunchback. Neutral Evil. Ideatum: *Might makes right and winner takes all.* I carry a rusted iron bar and two stone fists. The bar is for show. The fists are for everything else. I'm somewhere I haven't named yet. Haven't claimed yet either. But I'm here. The weight's in my legs. The world stopped noticing me. That changes now. What do I call this place?


**GM:** The culvert hums. Not the Lattice hum — the old electrical hum of the luminescent strips, each one a small colony of dormant gomix life slowly dying in the dark. You stand at the junction where the maintenance passage meets the sedimentation access, the smell of rust and old water in your nose, the weight of your satchel against your hip. You have three coins. You have a body that the world doesn't quite know how to read. You have the Ideatum burning in you — *might makes right and winner takes all* — and the drainage district has offered you a route.

Kesh is at the far end of the culvert, checking his roster. He hasn't looked your way yet. Corra is somewhere in the deeper passages with her measuring rod and her notebook, reading the walls. The water runs slow in its channel. The next heavy flow is three days out, maybe four.

What do you do?
