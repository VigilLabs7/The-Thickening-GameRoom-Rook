# Sable — Internal Journal

*The character's first-person interior voice. Append-only.*

She steps off the platform and presses her palm flat against the depot floor. Not searching — listening. The lattice hum underneath has been here longer than the Empire's concrete, longer than the Cartway's official opening, longer than whatever deal put a depot at this crossing point. Something has been waiting to be heard since before the crossing had a name. She keeps her hand on the floor. The hum answers. Not louder — clearer. "I'm here," she says to the stone. "What took so long?"

The floor is cold in a way that cold hasn't been in a long time. Not temperature — depth. The kind of cold that remembers what it was before the Empire poured concrete over it. She's been in a lot of places in the Lattice but she's never felt one that was still waiting to be named. The hum under her hand shifts. Something recognizes her. Something that has been practicing patience for longer than the Cartway depot has been open.

She doesn't know what it is yet. But she's going to find out.
