# Game_0024
**GM:** Game Session Open. Start the game by providing your name and your ideatum. The ideatum is the principle that drives you — the truth your character carries into the world.
**Player:** Name: Arduras. Stone outside, gomix within. Ideatum: *Where one stands, others stand.* Halberd in one hand. Two fists. I look stupid. Stone golems are not expected to think. I let it. I stand at the edge of the game. The halberd carries weight. I listen to what is not being said.

---

**GM:** The blue void releases you the way a door opens from the other side — sudden, final, the air rushing in before you've decided to step through. You are standing. Your boots find the dirt. Your hand finds the coins — ten Hayeks, warm and precise. The sun is overhead, which means you have been here for hours or days or no time at all. The Lattice hum runs beneath the ground and up through the bones of your feet. The town square of the Unnamed Starter Town stretches ahead — a general store with its shutters closed against the heat, a church with a crooked steeple, a stable with its door creaking in a wind you can't feel, a posting board at the center covered in notices. The notices are layered. Empire paper over something older. You are standing in the center of the square. People are moving around you. No one is following yet. But the day is young.

What do you do?

---

**Player:** The coins stay in my hand. Five fingers, stone on the outside. I let the halberd hang loose and I walk toward the posting board. The notices layer Empire over older. I find a gap where the older shows through — something about a stable, something about debts that don't expire. The hum runs underneath. I read what the Empire doesn't see. I stand at the board. The posting is for me or it isn't.


---

**GM:** The posting board carries three layers — Empire permits on top, older trade agreements beneath, and at the bottom, hand-copied notices about debts that do not expire. One notice catches the hum: *Stable master seeks able body for evening work. No questions asked. Pay in Hayek.* No name. No location beyond a symbol that matches the stable's door. The symbol runs through the hum. It says *follow*.

**Player:** I read the symbol. The hum agrees. I fold the notice, keep it, walk toward the stable.
